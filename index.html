Intro

Anime is the name given to the Japanese style of animation which has become a popular form of entertainment for those not in Japan but for people all over the world, soem popular anime include Naruto, One piece, Bleach, and Attack on Titan.

Some of my personal favorite animes are Dragon ball and One Piece the story of these anime interest me because the revolve around the concept of things that don't exist currently and the expansion of the characters struggles, powers, and desires throughout the story allows me to connect and understand them and have a greater passion to see the succeed

Genre

Anime do follow the typical genre such as action, comedy, horror, etc. However, there are some genre or stories that are generally reserved for that of anime such as isekai which are stories of being reincarnated into altenate worlds typically fantasy medival worlds

A principale special to genre of anime is that is can also represent the gender and age that might be more interested and represent in the show such as the shounen which is caters towards teen boys while the shoujo genre caters to teen girls.
